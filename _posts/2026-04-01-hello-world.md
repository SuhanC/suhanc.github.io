Welcome to my blog! I'm Su Han Cho, a PhD student at the Max Planck Institute of Psychiatry in Munich, working at the intersection of bioinformatics and psychiatry.

## What to Expect

This space is where I'll share things that don't quite fit into a paper or a CV — rough ideas, reading notes, tools I find useful, and occasional updates on what I'm working on.

Topics I plan to write about:

- **Bioinformatics & methods** — scRNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, network biology
- **Research life** — navigating a PhD, lessons learned, things I wish I knew earlier
- **Tools & code** — workflows, scripts, and software that have made my life easier
- **Miscellaneous** — books, thoughts, and anything else that seems worth writing down

## Why Write?

Writing forces clarity. Half the time I start a post thinking I understand something, and by the end I realize I didn't. If even one person finds something here useful, that's a bonus.

Stay tuned — and feel free to reach out if anything catches your interest!
